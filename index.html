THE HERITAGE

40 YEARS OF DETROIT GRIT

Founded by Dave 'Easy' Arzola—a man who was already customizing mini-bikes at age 11—our roots run as deep as the city's industrial soul. With over 30 years of Michigan Avenue mastery, Dave has defined what it means to build a machine with hand-crafted precision.

"We don't do bolt-on parts or cookie-cutter builds. From frame-up restorations to high-performance engine work, every bike that leaves our floor is a piece of Detroit history, hammered out with sweat, sparks, and precision."
